## Company Logo Concepts

### Prompt 1: Gaussian R

```
Minimalist logo design for an AI research company called "Radiance Labs." The letter R is formed by 3-4 overlapping soft Gaussian ellipses, each a different warm color: amber, gold, coral. Where the ellipses overlap, colors blend smoothly together through alpha compositing, creating intermediate hues. The ellipses have soft, feathered edges like probability density functions, not hard outlines. The overall shape clearly reads as the letter "R" but is composed entirely of these blended, semi-transparent Gaussian blobs. Clean white background. Modern, scientific aesthetic. No text. Vector logo style, flat design, suitable for favicon and large display.
```

### Prompt 2: Radiance Field

```
Minimalist abstract logo for an AI company called "Radiance Labs." A single bright point at the center emitting concentric rings of soft Gaussian glow radiating outward. The center is bright gold-white, the first ring is warm amber, the second ring is softer gold, the outer ring fades to transparent. No hard edges anywhere. Clean white background. Scientific and meditative feel. Suitable as a company logo. Vector style, minimal, no text.
```

### Prompt 3: Overlapping Splats

```
Minimalist abstract logo for an AI research lab called "Radiance Labs." Four to five soft Gaussian blobs arranged in a loose, organic cluster. ## Product Logo

### Prompt 5: Prism (for Radiance Prisma)

```
Minimalist product logo for "Radiance Prisma," a scientific visualization tool. A clean geometric triangular prism in thin dark gray outlines. A single beam of white light enters from the left side of the prism. On the right side, the beam refracts into 4-5 separate beams of warm spectral colors: amber, gold, coral, rose, soft violet. The refracted beams fan out at slightly different angles. The beams have soft Gaussian edges — not perfectly sharp lines, but slightly feathered like Gaussian distributions. The prism itself is minimal — just clean geometric outlines, not filled. White background. The overall feel is scientific, elegant, referencing both optics and data decomposition. No text. ## Style Notes for All Prompts

- **Color palette:** Warm spectrum only — amber (#F4A300), gold (#FFD700), coral (#FF6B6B), rose (#FF8FA3), soft peach (#FFCBA4). No cold blues, no neon greens.
- **Edges:** Always soft/feathered/Gaussian. Never hard or sharp (except prism geometry).
- **Background:** White for logos, dark charcoal (#1A1A2E) for hero images.
- **Feel:** Scientific journal meets warm illustration. Think Nature cover art, not SaaS landing page.
- **Avoid:** Gradients that look like generic tech branding. Robot/AI imagery. Circuit patterns. Anything cold or corporate.
